Synopsis

The Story

In the 23rd century, humanity thrives on Orbit Sixty-Six, a luminous refuge after Earth's fall. Astrophysicist Dr. Laura Rogers and her team, the wise physicist Gary and his son Peter, and her brilliant protégé Neiman uncover the existence of cosmic strings hidden in the plasma surrounding Earth. The strings carry a coded message that appears to be a call or a warning from another universe. When Laura leads a daring mission to decode the message, their vessel is drawn through a wormhole into a mirror dimension, where luminous beings claim to be the architects of life itself. What begins as a scientific expedition turns into a cosmic revelation: these entities feed on the souls of the living to sustain their existence. Betrayed by Neiman — revealed to be an advanced android designed to aid dark beings — Laura must face the collapse of both universes. Guided by faith in human consciousness and the energy of compassion, she and her allies embark on a desperate quest to restore balance between creation and destruction, light and darkness, and to prove that the human spirit can outshine even the void between worlds.

Characters

Main Characters

Laura Rogers

Visionary astrophysicist and emotional core of the story; intelligent, empathetic, and driven by reason and wonder.

Neiman

Laura's gifted protégé, later revealed as an android whose hidden mission threatens humanity's survival.

Gary

A grounded scientist who balances logic with moral clarity; father of Peter and Laura's closest ally.

Peter

A young scientist representing the courage and curiosity of the next generation.

Kelly and Matt Rogers

The parents of Laura, who were engaged in time travel and the saviors of humanity in the first book, The Glowing Earth.

Angelo

A luminous, human-like entity who becomes Laura's guide and ally. He embodies balance, compassion, and knowledge.

The Energy Beings of Light

Collective consciousness formed of pure energy, harmony, and wisdom. They guard the Source of all souls.

The Host

A powerful shape-shifting being of darkness who tempts Neiman with visions of power and control.
