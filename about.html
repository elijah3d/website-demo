About Me

Nad Wismar

I was born in Germany in a small village Wismar. That's why my pseudonym is Wismar and the first name is Nad, which is a shortened version of Nadia. I moved to Paris when I was 16 years old to start living my adult life and to work and socialize with different cultures. My first career was in fashion and modelling. I travelled to the USA, Japan, Argentina, Spain, Italy, Germany, Morocco, Belgium and France. Then I worked as a business assistant and a copywriter writing articles about art, fashion, and technology. I realised that writing gives me a feeling of self-fulfilment and a connection with something magical and powerful. I transfer this positive energy through my books to readers. Currently I live in the Netherlands and work at the company as a manager. One of the most inspirational place for me is Seychelles. Please feel free to contact me via email (nadwismar@gmail.com), TikTok (nad.wismar) or Telegram (nadwismarwriter).
