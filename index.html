About Me

Nad Wismar

I was born in Germany in a small village Wismar. That's why my pseudonym is Wismar and the first name is Nad, which is Nadia's shortened version. I moved to Paris when I was 16 years old to start living my adult life, and to work and socialize with different cultures.

My first career was in fashion and modeling. I traveled to the USA, Japan, Argentina, Spain, Italy, Germany, Morocco, Belgium and France. Then I worked as a business assistant and a copywriter writing articles about art, fashion, and technology.

I realised that writing gives me a feeling of self-fulfilment and a connection with something magical and powerful. I transfer this positive energy through my books to readers. Currently I live in the Netherlands and work at the company as a manager. One of the most inspirational place for me is Seychelles.

When Characters Come to Life

Writing My Story: The Journey Behind The Glowing Earth

"When I began writing The Glowing Earth, it felt as if the characters were calling out to me, asking for their stories to be told. Holding this book in my hands today, I am reminded that dreams, no matter how distant, are always worth pursuing.

From living across continents to finding my voice here in Oss, this novel marks the beginning of an extraordinary journey—one that blends imagination, hope, and adventure. The Glowing Earth is not just a story; it is a piece of my heart shared with readers who dare to dream and explore.

"It's as if the characters themselves asked me to write about them."
